Chocolate Brownies

Description

Delectable chocolate brownies with a crunchy outside and soft gooey inside.

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Sugar
  • Cocoa Powder
  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Melt the butter with sugar, salt and cocoa powder
  2. Add the vanilla and eggs
  3. Add the flour
  4. Bake them for 20-25 minutes
  5. Enjoy!
