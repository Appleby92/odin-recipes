Passionfruit Martini

Description

Enjoy the flavour of summer with this tasty homemade cocktail.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 Passionfruit Smoothie
  • 1/2 Cup Ice
  • 2 Measures Vanilla Vodka
  • 2 Measures Malibu Passionfruit
  • 2 Measures Prosecco

Steps

  1. Mix all ingredients into cocktail shaker
  2. Shake - holding tight
  3. Gently release pressure
  4. Fill up with lemonade
  5. Replace mesh lid to serve
  6. Enjoy!
