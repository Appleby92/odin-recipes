Recipe for Disaster

Description

Don't be deceived by the name nor the image, for the taste of this delicious dessert is anything but disastrous!

Ingredients

  • 200g / 8oz dark chocolate
  • 125g / 5oz butter
  • 3 tbsp golden syrup
  • 200g / 8oz digestive biscuits
  • 100g / 4oz mini marshmallows

Steps

  1. Grease tin
  2. Crush biscuits
  3. Melt chocolate, butter and golden syrup
  4. Add crushed biscuits and marshmallows to the chocolate sauce
  5. Pour mix into baking tin and place in the fridge for a few hours
  6. Cut into squares an Enjoy!
